    The Asaad Group began its journey in the minds of its founders since they started to work as employees in different establishments in various countries. Asaad is the epitome of the unity of three siblings; two brothers and their sister. Coming from a background of agriculture and hardship at a very young age, the founders have come a long way in establishing the Asaad Group as it is today. However, the actual establishment in Kerala revved up during the year 2008 when they began it through a small ‘Chayakada’, a local name for small tea shops, in Perumbavoor which eventually had quite an impressive response from the public.

    Passionate in providing quality food products under a trusted brand the group further established more of their ventures in Kalady and Perumbavoor through supermarkets, bakeries, and restaurants. The Group today is focussed on developing properties and providing space for every class of citizens, a place for spending their money on the exchange of good quality food products and services. Asaad strives to follow ethical business practices and has always been committed to the people and the nation. The Group aims at further establishing its presence all throughout the country through development and improvisation.
